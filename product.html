The Vortex Shell is our most advanced waterproof jacket. Built with 3-layer Gore-Tex Pro fabric and engineered for maximum performance in extreme conditions. Features fully sealed seams, YKK AquaGuard zippers, and laser-cut ventilation.

The Vortex Shell represents the pinnacle of performance outerwear engineering. Built with 3-layer Gore-Tex Pro fabric, it offers unmatched waterproof protection while maintaining exceptional breathability.

Key features include:

  • 3-layer Gore-Tex Pro construction (28,000mm waterproofing)
  • Fully sealed seams with taped construction
